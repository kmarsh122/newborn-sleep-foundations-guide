Common questions

You might be
wondering...

My baby is 9 weeks old. Is this the right time?

Yes. This plan is designed specifically for 8–12 week olds. Before 8 weeks, babies need to lead; after 8 weeks, their biology is ready for gentle structure. You're in the sweet spot. If your baby is already past 12 weeks, don't worry — we have plans built for exactly where they are now: check out The 3 Month Reset or The 4 Month Transition Plan.

Does this involve letting my baby cry it out?

No. This is not a sleep training plan. It's a scheduling and routine plan. We're building the foundation so that if you choose to sleep train later, your baby is already primed. For now, it's all about gentle consistency.

I'm breastfeeding. Does this still work?

Absolutely. The plan works for both breast and bottle feeding. The feeding frequency guidance is flexible, and nothing here requires you to change how you feed your baby.

What if my baby doesn't follow the schedule?

These are guidelines, not rules. The most important anchor is a consistent bedtime (8–8:30pm) and wake time (7:30–8am). Everything else adjusts around your baby. Kim built in flexibility throughout the plan, because real babies are not robots.

How quickly will I see results?

Most families start seeing longer night stretches within the first week of consistent implementation. The key is the bedtime anchor. Once that's established, nighttime naturally improves.
